Visión

We want you to always have at hand all the records of the learning you have acquired, whether it is when you read a book, take a course or simply want to save those ideas that arise in your daily life.
We don't want notes or materials to get lost between loose sheets, different applications or forgotten notebooks, but to allow you to always have your notes at hand and to help make connections with other people in the world, of the topics or learning acquired with them in order to generate a network of knowledge and ensure that your knowledge feeds back more and more.


What is Assisfolk

Is an expanding knowledge network that connects your ideas with the ideas of other people around the world.
This knowledge network allows people to connect based on related topics, and has a feedback system to expand your knowledge.
